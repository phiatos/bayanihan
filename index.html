Angat Pinas, Inc., commonly known as Angat Buhay, is a Filipino non-profit organization that aims to empower Filipinos to become communities of active citizens by mobilizing the largest volunteer network in implementing Bayanihan programs.

Angat Buhay Relief Operations

Angat Buhay operates nationwide, implementing programs, projects, and activities with partners and volunteers from different parts of Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, and focusing communities with limited access to education, nutrition and health services, as well as disaster-stricken areas.
